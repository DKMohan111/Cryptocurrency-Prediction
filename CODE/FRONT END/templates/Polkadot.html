About Informations

Polkadot (DOT) is a next-generation blockchain protocol designed to enable interoperability between multiple blockchains. Developed by the Web3 Foundation, Polkadot facilitates the transfer of any type of data or asset across different blockchains, creating a connected network of diverse blockchains. Its architecture consists of a central Relay Chain that coordinates consensus and communication among various Parachains (parallel chains) that can host their own tokens and optimized functionalities. This design enhances scalability and allows for specialized blockchains to interoperate seamlessly. The native token, DOT, serves multiple roles within the network, including governance, staking, and bonding.
